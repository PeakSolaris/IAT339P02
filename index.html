Branding Ideas

Cocomolo is a company that offers a wide range of accessories and jewelry, primarily targeted towards women but available for anyone to purchase. Our offerings include hair accessories, headwear, bags, and handmade jewelry that cater to a range of styles, including trendy, retro, and fun. Our products are designed to appeal to individuals who enjoy accessorizing with items such as rings, hair clips, and bags, and who are interested in fashion trends. Our handmade products showcase originality and authenticity, and our company is also committed to sustainability.

Colour Palette

#000000 – Black

 •  used for the main headings and paragraphs.

#df2c4d – Cherry Red

 •  used to highlight the item that is being hovered over.

#84a37e – Muted Green

 •  used for the header background and buttons.

#ffffff – White

 •  used for the background of the page because it provides brightness and adds vibrancy to the design.

The muted green and the white will induce a welcoming atmosphere. The red will give a pop of colour and can add a retro-style feel to the website.

Typefaces

 •  The main font is Lato by Łukasz Dziedzic. Lato not only exudes a friendly and inviting aura, but also conveys a sense of professionalism.

 •  Poppins is also used by Indian Type Foundy because it evokes a calm mood and the appearance isn't too overwhelming for the user.
